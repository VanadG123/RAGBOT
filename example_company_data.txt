# Example Company Knowledge Base

## Company Overview

TechCorp Solutions is a leading software development company founded in 2015, specializing in AI-powered business solutions. We serve over 500 enterprise clients worldwide and have offices in New York, London, and Singapore.

## Our Products

### AI Platform Suite
- **SmartAnalytics**: Advanced data analytics platform using machine learning
- **AutoFlow**: Workflow automation tool for business processes  
- **ChatBot Pro**: Enterprise-grade conversational AI solution
- **DataSync**: Real-time data integration and synchronization tool

### Pricing Plans
- Starter Plan: $99/month - Up to 5 users, basic features
- Professional Plan: $299/month - Up to 25 users, advanced analytics
- Enterprise Plan: $999/month - Unlimited users, custom integrations
- Custom Plan: Contact sales for large organizations
